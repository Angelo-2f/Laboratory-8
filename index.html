Ahjin Guild Portfolio

Welcome to the Ahjin Guild Portfolio! Here, We showcase some of our favorite S-Rank Raids.

Raid Title: Jeju Island Raid

Enemy: Ant Queen and Ant King Beru
Chapter: 96
Battle Description: In a fierce showdown, Sung Jin-Woo faces off against Beru, his former loyal Shadow Soldier who has evolved into a formidable opponent. The battle showcases Jin-Woo’s mastery of shadow manipulation and strategic prowess as he adapts to Beru's incredible speed and combat skills. Initially, Beru's fierce attacks push Jin-Woo to his limits, but as the fight progresses, Jin-Woo displays his resilience and resourcefulness, ultimately overpowering Beru with a brilliant combination of tactics and his unwavering will.
Key Features:
- High Stakes: The battle takes place in a dangerous S-rank gate, posing significant risks to the hunters involved.
- Character Development: Sung Jin-Woo evolves significantly during this battle, demonstrating his leadership and tactical skills.
- Awakening Powers: Jin-Woo experiences power awakenings, using his unique ability to summon shadows from defeated enemies.

Raid Title: S-Rank Gate in Japan

Enemy: Legia King of the giants and the Monarch of Beginning
Chapter: 138
Battle Description: In a monumental clash, Sung Jin-Woo, the Shadow Monarch, confronts the Monarch of Beginning, a being with god-like powers capable of manipulating time and space. As they battle, Jin-Woo showcases his unmatched combat skills, summoning his vast army of shadows to counter the Monarch’s devastating attacks. The fight is a spectacular display of agility and strategy, with Jin-Woo tapping into his full potential to overcome the Monarch’s overwhelming abilities. Ultimately, he delivers a decisive blow, solidifying his status as one of the most powerful being.
Key Features:
- Sung Jin-Woo's Challenges: Jin-Woo faces formidable challenges that push him to tap into his full potential as the Shadow Monarch.
- Climactic Battle: The arc culminates in an epic showdown between Jin-Woo and Legia, featuring high-stakes combat and strategic maneuvers.
- Character Growth: Sung Jin-Woo's character development is highlighted as he learns from each battle, setting the stage for future conflicts.
